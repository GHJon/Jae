Books

Sponsored by GSA - Start up an LGBTQ book club with your GSA to introduce new books to your members, or look for some reading on your own. Check out our list of Recommended Books appropriate for youth in GSAs.

Movies

Jae's Community Centers offer movie screenings during lunch and evenings. With the movies, they give you a good way to be knowledgeable about yourself and make you shine amongst the others. After the movie, we hold a discussion affterwards to educate your peers and others surrounding you. Anybody who participates in this program can get free DVDs about LGBT issues in diverse locations around the world.

There are discounted rated when you buy them at Scholastic and Barnes and Noble's. Learn about all the awesome educational films offered through the Respect for all Project.

Legal Resources

Students have legal rights in schools, which also includes protection from harassment and discrimination. You can also ask to create a Jae's or GSA club if you wish. Knowing your legal rights helps to create a safer enviromnent within your school. Here are the following rights that protect your rights...

Federal Equal Access Act

The Sexual Orientation Non-Discrimination Act (SONDA)

Dignity for All Students Act (DASA)
