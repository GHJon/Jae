Jonathan Rodriguez, Co-Founder

Hello! My name is Jonathan Rodriguez, Co-Founder of The Jae's Project. Throughout my entire life, I've always been very aware of the environment itself and how it's been evolving since I was born. However, as I was researching sexuality rights across the United States, I've been disturbed by the close-minded people who portray them as a disgrace to their families and life itself. I’ve always wanted to make a change in the community in some fashion, whatever it may be violence, discriminatory actions, declining architecture, etc. With the creation of this company, we’re aiming to help all those that are hindered by their society and give them a space where they can feel like themselves and express their true personalities
